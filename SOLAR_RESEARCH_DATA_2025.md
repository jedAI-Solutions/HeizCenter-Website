## BAFA Funding 2025 (Verified)

### Base Funding Rate
- **30%** of eligible costs
- Minimum investment: €2,000
- Managed by KfW since January 1, 2024

### Bonus Programs

#### 1. Klimageschwindigkeitsbonus (Climate Speed Bonus)
- **+20%** additional funding
- Applies when replacing old fossil fuel heating systems
- Available for gas, oil heating replacements

#### 2. Einkommensbonus (Income Bonus)
- **+30%** additional funding
- For households with taxable annual income under €40,000
- Can be combined with Climate Speed Bonus

### Maximum Total Funding
- **Up to 70%** of eligible costs
- Calculation: 30% (base) + 20% (speed) + 30% (income) = 80% theoretical
- BAFA caps maximum at **70%**

### Funding Examples

## Technical Specifications

### Collector Types

#### 1. Flachkollektoren (Flat Plate Collectors)
- **Efficiency:** 60-75%
- **Cost:** €300-500/m²
- **Best for:** Warmwasser systems, moderate heating support
- **Advantages:** Lower cost, proven technology, good for south-facing roofs
- **Disadvantages:** Lower efficiency in winter

#### 2. Vakuumröhrenkollektoren (Vacuum Tube Collectors)
- **Efficiency:** 70-85%
- **Cost:** €500-750/m²
- **Best for:** Kombi systems, year-round heating support
- **Advantages:** Higher efficiency, better winter performance, works with east/west orientation
- **Disadvantages:** Higher cost, more complex

### Storage Tank Sizes

#### Warmwasser (Hot Water Only)
- **2-3 persons:** 250-300L
- **4-5 persons:** 300-400L
- **6+ persons:** 400-500L

#### Kombi (Heating Support)
- **Solar storage:** 400-600L
- **Buffer storage:** 300-500L
- **Total combined:** 600-1,000L

### Collector Sizing Rules

**Warmwasser:**
- **Rule of thumb:** 1.0-1.5 m² per person
- **4-person household:** 4-6 m²

**Kombi System:**
- **Rule of thumb:** 1.0-1.5 m² per 10 m² living space
- **150 m² house:** 12-15 m²

## Solarthermie vs. Photovoltaik Comparison

### Solarthermie (Solar Thermal)
**Purpose:** Heat generation (hot water + heating support)

**Advantages:**
- Higher efficiency for heat (60-80% vs 15-22%)
- Lower installation cost per kWh heat generated
- Perfect complement to gas/oil heating
- Ideal for existing heating systems
- Direct heating support in spring/autumn

**Best For:**
- Hot water production
- Heating support (reduce gas/oil consumption)
- Existing conventional heating systems
- Homes without heat pump

**Efficiency:** 60-80% of sunlight → heat

### Photovoltaik (Solar PV)
**Purpose:** Electricity generation

**Advantages:**
- Versatile (electricity for everything)
- Can feed excess to grid
- Battery storage option
- Powers heat pumps
- Can charge e-vehicles

**Best For:**
- General electricity needs
- Heat pump operation
- E-mobility
- High electricity consumption
- Future-proofing energy independence

**Efficiency:** 15-22% of sunlight → electricity

### Hybrid Solution: Best of Both Worlds

**Solarthermie + Wärmepumpe:**
- Solarthermie provides direct heating support (spring/autumn)
- Reduces wärmepumpe electricity consumption
- Total system cost: ~€10,000 (solar) + WP
- Maximum energy efficiency
- Eligible for combined BAFA funding

**Solarthermie + PV + Wärmepumpe:**
- Complete energy independence
- PV powers heat pump
- Solarthermie provides direct heat
- Ultimate eco-friendly solution
- Highest upfront cost, best long-term savings
